Energy Flow

This panel illustrates how much power is entering and leaving the system in real time. The vertical bars show “Power In” and “Power Out,” giving users a simple visual of how efficiently energy is being transferred. The color-coded bar below helps identify whether the system is gaining or losing energy, providing an instant overview of overall performance.

AC Grid Tie

The AC Grid Tie module shows how the microgrid connects to the main electrical grid. It tracks voltage, current, and total power, ensuring a stable exchange between generated energy and the grid. The clean blue panel and organized layout make complex electrical data easy to understand at a glance.

Wind Data

The wind section captures energy produced by the lab’s wind turbines. It displays detailed readings like voltage, current, and total watt hours collected, helping users visualize how much power is being generated through wind. This feature promotes awareness of renewable energy diversity and shows how different energy sources work together to power the system.
